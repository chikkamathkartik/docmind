
Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

Artificial intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence 
processes by computer systems. These processes include learning, 
reasoning, and self-correction.

Machine learning is a subset of AI that provides systems the ability 
to automatically learn and improve from experience without being 
explicitly programmed.

Agentic RAG extends standard RAG by adding an autonomous agent layer 
that can decide which tools to use, when to search documents versus 
the web, and can perform multi-step reasoning before generating an answer.
